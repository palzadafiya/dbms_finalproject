Project Information

BillBuddy is designed to simplify the process of splitting expenses among groups of people, such as friends, roommates, or travelers. Its primary goal is to make it easy to share costs fairly and transparently, reducing the stress and complexity often associated with dividing bills.

Key Features

  • Expense Splitting: Allows users to input expenses and then divides them among group members equally.

  • Group Creation: Users can create groups, like for a household, a trip, or a dinner outing. This feature is especially useful for tracking shared expenses over time with a consistent group of people.

  • Debt Simplification: The app simplifies debts among a group. For example, if person A owes B $10, B owes C $5 and A owes C $10, BillBuddy will simply show that A owes B $5 and A owes C $15, making it easier to understand and settle debts.

  • Expense Tracking and History: Users can view their expense history, track who they owe money to, and see who owes them money. This includes detailed information on each expense.
